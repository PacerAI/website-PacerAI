Our Mission

Make revenue intelligence accessible

Every SaaS company deserves the same quality of revenue analytics that a $500M ARR public company has — regardless of whether they have a data team, a lakehouse, or a dedicated FP&A function. We exist to close that gap.

The Problem We Solve

Revenue data is broken

SaaS companies spend months building the infrastructure to answer basic revenue questions. It shouldn't be this hard.

The Old Way
  • ✕ ARR models built in fragile spreadsheets
  • ✕ Customer data scattered across 4–6 systems
  • ✕ Board decks assembled over weekends
  • ✕ Metrics that don't match between teams
  • ✕ 3–6 months to build basic reporting
  • ✕ Stale by the time it reaches the board
The Pacer AI Way
  • ✓ Automated ARR Snowball from live data
  • ✓ Unified Customer Data Cube across all sources
  • ✓ Board-ready reports generated on demand
  • ✓ One semantic model, one source of truth
  • ✓ Operational in 2–4 weeks
  • ✓ Always current, always defensible
Founder

Built by someone who lived the problem

WS

Will Sullivan

Founder & CEO

Will spent over a decade building revenue data infrastructure for PE-backed SaaS companies — the kind of work that involves reconciling CRM exports, rebuilding ARR waterfalls every quarter, and explaining to boards why the numbers changed since last month.

After watching the same pattern repeat across dozens of companies — smart teams, burning months to produce basic revenue analytics that should already exist — he built Pacer AI to make institutional-grade revenue intelligence automatic.

Pacer AI combines Will's deep domain expertise in SaaS metrics and financial modeling with the modern data stack (Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, AI agents) to deliver what used to take a team of four in a fraction of the time.

What We Stand For

Principles that shape the product

🎯

Precision Over Volume

We'd rather get five metrics exactly right than build fifty dashboards nobody trusts. Every number in Pacer AI is traceable back to its source transaction. If it can't be defended in a board meeting, it doesn't ship.

🏗️

Infrastructure, Not Band-Aids

We build semantic models and data foundations — not another spreadsheet layer. The goal is a revenue data asset that compounds in value over time, not a report you have to rebuild every quarter.

🤝

Meet Finance Where They Work

Finance teams live in Excel, PowerPoint, and Power BI. We deliver intelligence into those tools — not into another portal they have to learn. Adoption happens when the tool fits the workflow, not the other way around.

🔒

Trust Is Non-Negotiable

Your revenue data is the most sensitive information in the company. Pacer AI runs on Microsoft Fabric inside your own Azure tenant. Your data never leaves your control. Zero exceptions.
